Frequently Asked Questions

How does Meta advertising differ from Google Ads?

Google Ads primarily targets users actively searching for products or services (intent-based), while Meta Ads target users based on interests, behaviors, and demographics (interest-based). Google Ads works well when users know what they're looking for, while Meta Ads excel at creating demand and awareness. Both platforms can be highly effective when used strategically, and often work best when used together in a comprehensive digital marketing strategy.

What's a reasonable budget for Meta advertising?

The ideal budget depends on your industry, competition, and goals. For testing and initial campaigns, we recommend a minimum of ₹50,000 per month in ad spend (plus management fee) to gather sufficient data for optimization. For established businesses looking for significant results, budgets typically start at ₹1,00,000+ per month. We'll help you determine the optimal budget based on your specific business objectives during our strategy consultation.

How long before I see results from Meta ads?

While you may see initial results quickly, Meta campaigns typically require a learning period of 1-2 weeks as the algorithm optimizes delivery. During the first month, we focus on testing different audiences, creatives, and strategies to identify what works best for your business. Most clients see significant improvements in the second month as we refine and scale the winning strategies. For e-commerce and lead generation campaigns, we often achieve good results within 4-6 weeks.

Do you create the ad creative and copy?

Yes, our team handles the creation of ad copy and basic design elements as part of our service. For more complex video production or professional photoshoots, we can either work with your existing assets or connect you with our network of trusted creative partners at additional cost. We'll discuss your creative needs and capabilities during our onboarding process.

How do you measure the success of Meta ad campaigns?

We measure success based on your specific business goals, which might include metrics like return on ad spend (ROAS), cost per acquisition (CPA), lead quality, conversion rate, and overall revenue growth. We provide detailed reports showing both the direct performance metrics from Meta as well as the impact on your overall business objectives. We use advanced tracking and analytics to ensure accurate attribution and provide actionable insights.
