Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services

What services does Digital Service offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of digital marketing services including Google Ads, Meta Ads (Facebook and Instagram), Website Development, and Full Funnel Tracking. Our team specializes in creating customized strategies to help businesses grow their online presence and generate measurable results.

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies based on the scope and requirements of each project. We offer customized packages tailored to your business needs and budget. Contact us for a free consultation to discuss your specific requirements and get a detailed quote.

How long does it take to see results from digital marketing campaigns?

The timeline for seeing results varies depending on factors such as your industry, competition, current online presence, and the specific marketing channels used. Generally, you can expect to see initial results from paid advertising campaigns within a few weeks, while SEO and content marketing efforts typically take 3-6 months to show significant results.

Do you offer long-term contracts?

We believe in earning your business through results, not contracts. While we recommend a minimum commitment period to properly implement and optimize your campaigns, we don't lock clients into long-term contracts. Our agreements typically run on a month-to-month basis after the initial period.

How do you measure and report on campaign performance?

We provide detailed monthly reports that track key performance indicators (KPIs) relevant to your business goals. Our reporting includes metrics such as website traffic, conversion rates, cost per acquisition, return on ad spend, and more. We also schedule regular review calls to discuss performance, insights, and strategy adjustments.
