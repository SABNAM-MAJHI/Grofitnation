Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I see results from Google Ads?

Google Ads typically begins driving traffic immediately after campaigns are approved, which usually takes less than 24 hours. However, optimization for peak performance generally takes 2-4 weeks as we gather data and refine the campaigns based on initial results. The timeline for ROI can vary depending on your industry, competition, and the quality of your website/landing pages.

What's the minimum budget required for Google Ads?

While there's technically no minimum spend required by Google, we recommend a starting budget of at least ₹30,000 per month in ad spend (in addition to our management fee) to gather sufficient data for optimization. The ideal budget varies by industry and location, with more competitive industries requiring higher budgets. We'll provide a custom recommendation based on your specific business and goals during our initial consultation.

How do you measure the success of Google Ads campaigns?

We measure success through key performance indicators (KPIs) directly tied to your business goals. These typically include return on ad spend (ROAS), cost per acquisition (CPA), conversion rate, quality score, and impression share. We establish baseline metrics at the beginning of our engagement and track improvements over time. Our reporting focuses on the metrics that matter most to your bottom line, not just vanity metrics like clicks or impressions.

Do I own my Google Ads account?

Absolutely. We set up and manage your campaigns through your own Google Ads account, which you maintain full ownership of. We simply request administrative access to manage the campaigns on your behalf. If you already have an account, we'll use that. If not, we'll create one for you that remains your property even if our relationship ends.

What makes your Google Ads management different from other agencies?

Our approach is defined by three key differentiators:

  1. Data-driven optimization: We make decisions based on comprehensive data analysis, not guesswork, with continuous A/B testing of ads, keywords, and landing pages.
  2. Transparent reporting: You'll always know exactly how your campaigns are performing with clear, jargon-free reports focused on business outcomes.
  3. Specialized expertise: Our Google Ads specialists are certified experts who focus exclusively on PPC, staying at the cutting edge of Google's ever-evolving features and best practices.

How often will my account be optimized?

The frequency of optimization depends on your plan level and ad spend. Our Professional and Enterprise plans include weekly or daily optimizations, while our Starter plan includes bi-weekly optimizations. However, we monitor all accounts regularly for any critical issues regardless of plan level. Common optimization activities include bid adjustments, keyword refinement, ad copy testing, negative keyword addition, and audience targeting adjustments.

Can you guarantee results with Google Ads?

While we can't guarantee specific results due to the many variables involved (including market conditions, competition, website quality, and industry), we do guarantee our expertise and commitment to achieving the best possible outcomes. We're transparent about what's realistic based on your industry and budget, and we establish clear KPI targets at the beginning of our engagement. Our track record demonstrates consistent success across diverse industries, and we're confident in our ability to improve your Google Ads performance.

Do I need a new website before starting Google Ads?

Not necessarily, but the quality of your website or landing pages significantly impacts Google Ads performance. We'll evaluate your current website and make recommendations for improvements if needed. In some cases, we may suggest creating dedicated landing pages for your campaigns to maximize conversion rates. Our Website Development service can help with these improvements if required, but it's not mandatory to start with Google Ads.
