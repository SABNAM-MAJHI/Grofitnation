Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for website development varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. A basic website typically takes 3-4 weeks, a business website around 6-8 weeks, and an e-commerce website can take 8-12 weeks. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide website hosting and maintenance?

Yes, we offer website hosting and maintenance services to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and performs optimally. Our maintenance packages include regular updates, security monitoring, backup services, and technical support. We can discuss these options during our consultation.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites we develop are fully responsive and optimized for all devices - desktops, tablets, and smartphones. With mobile traffic accounting for over 50% of web traffic, we ensure your website provides an excellent user experience regardless of the device your visitors are using.

Will I be able to update the website myself?

Yes, we develop websites with user-friendly content management systems (CMS) that allow you to easily update content, add pages, and manage basic website elements without technical knowledge. We also provide training sessions to ensure you're comfortable managing your website after launch.

How do you ensure my website will generate leads or sales?

We design websites with conversion in mind from the start. This includes strategic placement of call-to-action elements, optimized user journeys, persuasive content, and lead capture mechanisms. We also implement proper analytics tracking to measure performance and continuously improve conversion rates over time.
